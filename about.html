Since 2005, 360 Degrees Production House has turned good ideas into larger-than-life event productions. We build our events from the ground up – layer by layer, detail by detail – so they leave a lasting impression that’s impossible to replicate.

We don’t just show up — we own the spotlight.

Our team transforms big ideas into unforgettable moments with fearless creativity and flawless execution — from launches and award shows to expos, roadshows, and beyond — delivering experiences that engage, inspire, and resonate. We’re a trusted partner to leading brands, crafting impact-driven events for audiences across industries and borders.
